# CrowdReply

AI search visibility platform — get brands mentioned in LLM responses via Reddit marketing and citation optimization.

## Overview

CrowdReply monitors where your brand appears in AI-generated responses from major LLM providers (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude) and optimizes your visibility through strategic Reddit engagement. Track mentions, optimize citations, and amplify your brand's presence in AI search results.

## Key Features

- **Brand Mention Tracker** - Real-time monitoring of brand mentions across Reddit, LLM responses, and citation sources
- **AI Visibility Score** - Dashboard showing 0–100 visibility score across LLM providers with daily snapshots
- **Automated Reply Engine** - Generate context-aware Reddit replies optimized for citation signals
- **Reddit Thread Discovery** - Automatically discover high-relevance threads ranked by authority
- **Competitor Mention Comparison** - Side-by-side analysis of competitor visibility and share-of-voice
- **Citation Source Attribution** - Break down which LLM and source URLs are citing your brand
- **Campaign Manager** - Create targeted mention campaigns with budget caps and scheduling
- **Weekly Visibility Report** - Automated email digest with trends and recommended actions

## How It Works

### 1. Monitor
CrowdReply continuously monitors major LLM providers and Reddit conversations to detect your brand mentions.

### 2. Analyze
Get visibility scores, competitor comparisons, and citation source breakdowns in real-time.

### 3. Optimize
Use the Automated Reply Engine to engage strategically on Reddit and amplify citation signals.

### 4. Measure
Track ROI per campaign with detailed analytics and weekly visibility reports.

## API Endpoints

- `GET /api/visibility-score` - Current visibility metrics across LLMs
- `GET /api/mentions` - Brand mention tracking and history
- `GET /api/reddit-threads` - Discovered Reddit threads for engagement
- `POST /api/campaigns` - Create and manage mention campaigns
- `GET /api/competitors` - Competitor mention analysis
